Terms summary

Form submissions and WhatsApp order requests are treated as requests until the restaurant team confirms availability, timing, and any event-specific requirements.

Reservations

Table bookings remain subject to confirmation

  • Table requests are subject to seating availability.
  • Large-group bookings may require direct confirmation by phone.
  • Please inform the restaurant quickly if your plans change.
Catering

Packages may change based on service scope

  • Pricing and menu scope may vary by guest count, venue, travel distance, and service style.
  • Custom event work may require an advance discussion before final confirmation.
  • Buffet or large-event planning may need additional lead time for coordination.
Orders

WhatsApp checkout supports the final order handoff

  • WhatsApp checkout sends the final order summary to the restaurant for confirmation.
  • Delivery or kitchen timing shown on the site is an estimate and may vary during peak periods.
  • Availability may change for limited dishes or same-day high-demand platters.
