Privacy summary

Information shared through the website is used only to respond to customer requests, coordinate service, and support the dining or catering experience guests asked for.

What We Collect

Information you choose to share

  • Name, phone number, email, and booking details submitted through reservation or enquiry forms.
  • Event details, guest counts, and menu preferences submitted through catering forms.
  • Basic technical information sent by your browser when a form is submitted.
How It Is Used

Only for restaurant operations and follow-up

  • To confirm reservations, group bookings, and catering requests.
  • To respond to enquiries and follow up on dining or order requests.
  • To share offers or updates only when a guest asked for them.
Storage & Access

Handled for operational follow-up

Submitted form details may be stored on the hosting server used for the website so the restaurant team can review and respond. Access should remain limited to authorized administrators or the restaurant team handling requests.

Your Choices

Ask us to stop promotional follow-up

If you no longer want updates, you can request that the restaurant stop using your contact details for promotional communication. For support, call +91 77977 77773 or use the contact options on the main website.
